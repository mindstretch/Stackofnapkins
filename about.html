About

We started this
on a napkin.

That's actually true. Stack of Napkins came out of building our own products — a wellness app, a reputation management tool — and realizing that the most valuable thing we'd built wasn't the products themselves. It was the agents running them.

The onboarding agent that handled 800+ signups without a single manual email. The review agent that monitored three platforms, drafted responses, and posted them — while we were doing other things. The briefing agent that told us what happened overnight so we didn't have to check.

"We built AI that ran our business. Then we realized we could build it for yours."

Most businesses aren't missing a strategy. They're missing bandwidth. The same 20 tasks that eat half their week — the inbox management, the follow-ups, the review responses, the internal reporting — are exactly the kind of work an agent is good at. Repetitive, rule-based, time-consuming, and easy to forget.

We're not an AI consultancy. We don't do strategy decks. We build agents, deploy them, and run them. The name is intentional — great ideas start on napkins. Ours just happen to run on AI.

How we work.

01

Build, don't consult

We don't sell strategy. We sell working agents. If we can't build it, we say so. If we can, we do it fast and show you something real.

02

Earn autonomy over time

Agents start cautious. They escalate. They check. Over time, as they prove themselves, they earn more independence. We don't deploy and disappear.

03

You stay in control

The agent works for your business. You approve what matters, override what doesn't feel right, and get briefed on everything. It's your name on the emails.

04

Honest about what AI can't do

Some things need a human. We'll tell you which ones. An agent that tries to do everything usually does nothing well. Scope matters.

05

Move fast, test first

Weeks, not months. But nothing goes live untested. We move quickly because we've done the work before — not because we're cutting corners.

06

We eat our own cooking

We run agents on our own products. If we wouldn't deploy something for ourselves, we won't deploy it for you. That keeps us honest.

Who builds this.

Small team. Big surface area. We move fast because we keep it tight.
